    Everything you need to know about our programs

    Do I need prior coding experience to join?

    No prior experience required! Our programs are designed for complete beginners. We start with fundamentals and gradually build up to advanced concepts with hands-on practice.

    Will I get placement assistance?

    Yes! We provide full placement assistance including resume reviews, mock interviews, LinkedIn profile optimization, and direct referrals to our hiring partner network.

    What is the duration and schedule of the courses?

    Our courses run for 16 weeks with live classes Monday to Friday, 7–9 PM. Weekend batch options are also available.

    What kind of projects will I work on?

    You'll work on industry-grade capstone projects like churn prediction models, sales forecasting dashboards, fraud detection systems, and more—building a portfolio that showcases real skills.

    Are the instructors industry professionals?

    Absolutely. All instructors have 5+ years of industry experience at top tech companies and are carefully vetted for both technical expertise and teaching ability.

    What certifications will I receive?

    You'll receive a Boffins Academy course completion certificate and, depending on your course, industry-recognized partner certifications that are valued by employers.
