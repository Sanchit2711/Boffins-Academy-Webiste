Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our programs

Do I need prior coding experience to join?

No prior experience required. We start from fundamentals and build up to advanced concepts with practical projects.

Will I get placement assistance?

Yes. You get resume support, mock interviews, portfolio reviews, and hiring partner referrals.

What is the duration and schedule of the courses?

Durations vary by track and include flexible weekday/weekend slots for students and working professionals.

What kind of projects will I work on?

You will build capstone and portfolio projects based on real industry workflows.

Are the instructors industry professionals?

Yes, mentors are experienced practitioners with strong product and engineering backgrounds.

What certifications will I receive?

You receive completion certification and track-specific validation based on assessment outcomes.
